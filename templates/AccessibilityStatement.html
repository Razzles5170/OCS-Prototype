Accessibility Statement

ROLSA Technologies is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We aim to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

We are actively working to improve our website's accessibility and usability, and we welcome feedback on how we can enhance the experience for all users.

Feel free to Contact Us if you have any questions or suggestions regarding the accessibility of our website.
